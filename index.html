The Team

This is team Puppy Love. The challenge was to design a real-world solution to a problem that people face in their everyday lives.

The idea for Puppy Love developed out of a round of brainstorming. We collected the ideas that we collectively were most passionate about, and collaborated on dot voting. The idea of a dog-matching app was too strong and, alas, Puppy Love was born!

Context

There are more dog owners now than ever before in human history. Over half of American households own at least one dog, and the pandemic saw an increase in the puppy population. Socializing your dog can be scary and unpleasant. We wanted to create a more enjoyable experience for all!
